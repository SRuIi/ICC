Quantitative trading

but is still in a groping stage in the digital currency market. Part assets of ICC will be used to carry out a dozen different digital currency strategy including index arbitrage, hedge arbitrage and high frequency arbitrage so as to ensure the stable earnings in the bullish or bearish market.
