ICC purchase tutorial

2017-12-27

ICC is a intelligent contract which is deployed on Ethernet's main chain, according transfer to contract address, ETH can gain corresponding ratio of ICC, now the first ICO is in progress, at this time, the ratio is 1 ETH ＝ 2000000 ICC.

At present, the normal way of ICO in market, such as sale token by websites, artificial token selling, are not quite formal. According to contract address, the artificial contract token sending way as automatically real-time exchange is the most formal one.

Purchase option

The ICC in a corresponding proportion can be obtained through sending ETH to the Ethernet contract address（0x027b6094ac3da754fccc7c088be04ca155782a66）.When you get ETH, ICC will reach into your account automatically in 3 mins.

About buying ETH, here's two ways ( processing fee of gate.io is 0.2％, it needs to bind bank card and verification. Bityear's processing fee is 2%, easy operating, accept Alipay.)

You must rember the sign in password and trading password！

Then do the security setting.

Purchase token

First recharge USTD into account(this exchange needs verification to start trading, before you buying ETH, please follow the request to set and verify.)

2. Purchase from Bityear（https://www.bityear.com）

Sign up an account and sign in, choose "one-click purchase"

Input the amount of ETH you want to purchase, press next.
